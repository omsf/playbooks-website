title: Alignment with OMSF
what: >
      OMSF requires that hosted projects:
      - Work on a problem within or related to the molecular sciences domain
      - License their work under an [OSI approved license](https://opensource.org/licenses)
      - Make their code publically available
      - Have a clear governance model
why: >
     OMSF works toward a vision of open source and open science. We also strive to create a sustainable community of mutually supportive experts.
importance: Required
when: Pre-sponsorship
