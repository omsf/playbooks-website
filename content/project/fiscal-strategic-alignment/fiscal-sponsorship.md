title: Fiscal Sponsorship
what: >
      Your funding has to be processed by some legal entity with the capacity to accept donations (from industry) or receive grants (from government agencies or philanthropic foundations), hire, pay salaries and other expenses. A fiscal sponsor has organizational capacity to deal with these matters, such as accountants, grants management, legal representation, and other specialized expertise in operations.
why: >
     Fiscal sponsorship spares each project from incurring substantial overhead costs on matters that are generally uninteresting to scientific developers, including tax management, managing HR and accounting requirements, and financial reporting.
implementation: OMSF
importance: High
when: >
      Pre-sponsorship (preferably pre-launch). The sooner, the better. If you already have a funded project, it’s not too late to seek fiscal sponsorship from OMSF, but if you have not yet launched your project, ensuring alignment and creating governance structures can be much smoother if you launch under fiscal sponsorship.
